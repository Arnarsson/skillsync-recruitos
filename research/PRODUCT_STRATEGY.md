## 2. H1 2026 Roadmap: EU AI Act Compliance = Product Features

### Phase 1: MVP Compliance (Feb-Mar, 4 weeks)
**Goal:** Close easy gaps, start documentation.

**Features to Ship:**
1. **Enhanced Audit Logs** (2 days)
   - Add `decision_type` field
   - Store full prompts + responses (not just hashes)
   - Add candidate notification tracking
   
2. **Technical Documentation** (5 days)
   - System description (Annex IV)
   - Model provenance documentation
   - Data governance policy

3. **Consent Tracking** (2 days)
   - Add consent_obtained field to candidate records
   - Consent UI component for LinkedIn extension

**Dev Effort:** 9 days (~2 weeks @ 1 dev)

### Phase 2: Core Compliance (Mar-Apr, 6 weeks)
**Goal:** Build critical missing features - human oversight, explainability, risk management.

**Features to Ship:**

1. **Human Review Queue** (5 days) - **CRITICAL**
   - Dashboard: Pending AI Assessments
   - Recruiter reviews score before it's "official"
   - Log: original_score, reviewed_by, review_timestamp, decision
   - Status: pending_review ‚Üí approved ‚Üí rejected
   - **Product value:** Prevents bad hires, adds quality gate

2. **Score Override with Justification** (3 days) - **CRITICAL**
   - Recruiter can adjust AI score (up or down)
   - Required: written justification (min 20 chars)
   - Log: override_reason, original_score, new_score
   - **Product value:** Handles edge cases, builds recruiter trust

3. **"AI Assisted" Badges** (1 day) - **CRITICAL**
   - Every AI-generated section labeled visibly
   - Hover tooltip: "Generated by AI. Verify independently."
   - **Product value:** Transparency builds trust

4. **ScoreExplainer Narratives** (4 days) - **CRITICAL**
   - Convert existing `ScoreExplainer` to natural language
   - "This candidate scored 85% because..."
   - Show which factors drove score up/down
   - **Product value:** EU requirement + helps recruiters understand AI

5. **Automation Bias Warning** (1 day)
   - Detect: recruiter accepts ALL AI recommendations
   - Show: "You've approved 10 consecutive AI scores. Review independently."
   - **Product value:** Prevents over-reliance

6. **Risk Management Register** (2 days)
   - Document risks: GitHub gender bias, hallucination risk, data staleness
   - Mitigation strategies for each
   - **Product value:** Transparency, proactive risk management

**Dev Effort:** 16 days (~3 weeks @ 1 dev)

### Phase 3: Full Compliance (Apr-May, 6 weeks)
**Goal:** Bias detection, candidate transparency, data governance.

**Features to Ship:**

1. **Bias Detection Dashboard** (10 days) - **CRITICAL**
   - Detect: gender/nationality/age disparities in scores
   - Alert if protected characteristics correlate with scores
   - Monthly fairness reports
   - **Product value:** Prevents discrimination lawsuits

2. **Candidate Transparency Portal** (7 days) - **CRITICAL**
   - Candidates can view AI assessment of them
   - Request human review
   - Challenge inaccurate data
   - **Product value:** Builds candidate trust, legal requirement

3. **Data Governance Dashboard** (3 days)
   - Show: what data collected, from where, retention period
   - GDPR deletion workflows
   - **Product value:** Privacy compliance, customer trust

4. **Model Performance Monitoring** (4 days)
   - Track: AI accuracy over time
   - Detect: model drift
   - Alert: when retraining needed
   - **Product value:** Quality control

**Dev Effort:** 24 days (~5 weeks @ 1 dev)

### Phase 4: Testing & Validation (May-Jun, 4 weeks)
**Goal:** Validate compliance, pen-test, external audit.

**Activities:**
1. Internal compliance audit (5 days)
2. Penetration testing (5 days)
3. External legal review (10 days)
4. Bug fixes from audit (10 days)

**Dev Effort:** 12 days + legal costs

### Phase 5: Conformity Assessment (Jun-Jul, 4 weeks)

## 11. Key Decision Points

### Decision 1: Compliance-First vs Feature-First?
**Recommendation:** Compliance-first.
- **Why:** Aug 2026 deadline is immovable
- **Why:** Every compliance feature = product feature
- **Why:** First-mover marketing advantage

### Decision 2: Nordic-Only vs Pan-European?
**Recommendation:** Nordic-only through 2026.
- **Why:** Focus wins, 13.5K shortage validates demand
- **Why:** TeamTailor integration = Nordic-specific advantage
- **Why:** Expand from strength after compliance proven

### Decision 3: Build vs Buy (ATS Integration)?
**Recommendation:** Build TeamTailor integration, partner for others.
- **Why:** TeamTailor = 11K Nordic companies
- **Why:** Their API is public and well-documented
- **Why:** Greenhouse/Workday require partnerships anyway

### Decision 4: Freemium vs Paid-Only?
**Recommendation:** Freemium with 5 analyses/month.
- **Why:** Lowers trial friction
- **Why:** Viral growth (recruiters share reports)
- **Why:** Credit consumption drives upgrades naturally
